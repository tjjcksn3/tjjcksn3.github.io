About Me

Hello! I'm Tiereney, a student at the University of Memphis that has a growing passion for UX design and research. This passion began from a curiosity of how people think and why people make the decisions that they do when interacting with UI. Diving into people's perspectives helps me to create and improve different designs. It helps to cater to those that experience my desgins in the best ways possible. Creating the best version of user experience is one of my biggest goals, so I am always open to learning new things.
